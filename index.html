The place to read

Prologue is an online digital library housing thousands of books, novels, and more. It enables you to purchase or rent books and read them on your device of choice at any time.
Organize your own library by adding books to your favorites or save them for later.

Anytime, anywhere

Prologue is available on your favorite device and platform so you can read wherever you want, whenever.

Easy on your eyes

Easily choose and set your favorite reading mode from One-page or Two-page view to the background color of the pages. Switch to light, sepia, or a dark background depending on your mood or time of the day.
